Med Test

The services collectively reflect ABC Laboratories' commitment to delivering comprehensive and accurate diagnostic solutions to meet the healthcare needs of its patients.

Blood Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Blood Chemistry Panel (including glucose, cholesterol, and electrolytes)

Blood Type and Rh Factor

Coagulation Tests (e.g., PT, INR, PTT)

Urinalysis

Examination of urine for abnormalities, such as protein, glucose, and cells.

Imaging Tests

X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, and ultrasounds for visualizing internal structures and identifying abnormalities.

Microbiology Tests

Cultures for bacteria, viruses, and fungi.

Sensitivity testing to determine the effectiveness of antibiotics.

Liver Function Tests

Assessing liver enzymes and other markers for liver health.

Genetic Tests

Assessing genetic markers for hereditary conditions and risk factors.
